Product Marketing Manager

Bettermode Remote (US) Full-time

About the role

Bettermode is looking for a Product Marketing Manager to join our growing team. You will be responsible for developing and executing marketing strategies to increase awareness and adoption of our products. This role requires a blend of creativity, analytical skills, and a deep understanding of the SaaS market.

Responsibilities

  • Develop product positioning and messaging that differentiates our products in the market.
  • Lead go-to-market strategies for new features and product launches.
  • Create compelling marketing content, including blog posts, case studies, and website copy.
  • Conduct market research and competitor analysis to identify opportunities.
  • Collaborate with product, sales, and customer success teams to ensure alignment.

Qualifications

  • 3+ years of experience in product marketing, preferably in a B2B SaaS company.
  • Proven track record of launching successful products.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, collaborative environment.
